About My Country (Bangladesh)

Bangladesh, a South Asian nation, is known for its lush green landscape crisscrossed by rivers. It's home to the world's largest delta, the Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for the Royal Bengal Tiger. Bustling Dhaka, the capital, boasts historic Mughal architecture like the Lalbagh Fort.


    Historical places of bangladesh

  1. Lalbagh Fort.
  2. Ahsan Manzil.
  3. Panam City.
  4. Mahasthangarh.
  5. Shat Gambuj Mosque.


About My City (Kushtia, Bangladesh)

Nestled within this vibrant country there's a city called Kushtia, a city known for its rich cultural heritage and literary legacy. Lalon Shah, a renowned mystic poet, hailed from Kushtia, and his shrine attracts pilgrims seeking spiritual solace. The city also boasts historical sites like Shilaidaha Kuthibari, a sprawling mansion once residence to Rabindranath Tagore's family.


    Historical places of Kushtia city

  1. Shilaidaha Kuthibari.
  2. Lalon Shah Mazar.
  3. Tagore Lodge.
  4. Jhaudia Shahi Mosque.
  5. Kushtia District Museum.
